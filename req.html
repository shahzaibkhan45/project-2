Requirments - Web Design and Development

We are looking for individuals who have a strong foundation and interest in web design and development. The ideal candidate should have a good understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as these are the core technologies used to build modern and responsive websites. You should be able to create clean, user-friendly, and visually appealing web pages that work smoothly on all devices including desktop, tablet, and mobile. In addition, knowledge of responsive design principles, layout structuring, and basic UI/UX concepts is highly important. The candidate should be capable of converting design ideas into fully functional websites while maintaining good coding practices and attention to detail. Familiarity with tools such as version control (Git), and understanding of basic frontend frameworks or libraries will be considered a plus. Problem-solving skills, creativity, and the ability to learn new technologies quickly are also highly valued. Overall, we are seeking someone who is passionate about building modern websites and is eager to grow their skills in the field of web development and design.
